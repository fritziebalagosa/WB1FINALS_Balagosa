About Include

Include was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to create equal employment opportunities for people with disabilities by connecting them with inclusive employers.

Our team consists of disability advocates, HR professionals, and tech experts who understand both the challenges faced by people with disabilities in the job market and the benefits of building diverse teams.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to find meaningful work where they can thrive, and that employers benefit immensely from diverse perspectives and experiences.
